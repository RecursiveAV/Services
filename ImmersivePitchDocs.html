01. Who we are

Company Overview

We are a multi-disciplined design studio creating real‑world experiences to tell your story and connect you with your audience through audiovisual design, content creation and bespoke software development.

We design technology solutions across AV, Lighting, Content and Software to transform spaces for brands, venues, and people.

We are always happy to take a leadership role in all matters relating to technology systems planning and design, as well as closely coordinating with the wider project team leading other technology, infrastructure, interior and interfacing design areas.

We limit our portfolio so that our team are involved in every project, they discuss each project and often work together on them. This ensures that we are flexible and nimble and are able to think creatively without being restrained or bogged down in bureaucracy or process.

Our reputation and integrity are of paramount importance to us as a team and embedded in everything we do. The majority of our business is won by recommendations and referrals as a reflection of this. We embrace critical thinking, curiosity, and a respectful approach to challenge the status quo. This mindset keeps us conscious of our commitment to understanding and the potential to improve existing norms.

02. Market Insights

Industry trends

The immersive technology market, encompassing VR, AR, and MR, is forecasted to reach $169.24 billion by 2030, driven by advancements in hardware and software, and their application across sectors like gaming, healthcare, and education​

Gaming leads the sector with VR and AR providing enhanced experiences. Education and training also see significant adoption, offering interactive and engaging learning environments. Healthcare benefits from immersive technologies in areas like surgical training and patient therapy​.

Technological advancements, particularly in hardware like headsets and sensors, are crucial for delivering immersive experiences. Generative AI is increasingly used for efficient content creation, making immersive environments more accessible​.

The rise of remote work is boosting XR collaboration tools, enabling virtual meetings and project collaboration. Companies are enhancing customer experiences through virtual showrooms and remote support, driving further adoption of immersive technologies​​.



Target Audience:



•  

The gaming industry is the largest segment for immersive technologies, driven by VR and AR enhancements that provide lifelike visuals and interactive experiences. This sector is highly relevant due to its continuous demand for more engaging and realistic gaming environments, catering to a tech-savvy audience looking for advanced sensory experiences​.

•  

Education and Training Immersive technologies are increasingly used in education and training, providing realistic simulations and interactive learning environments. These technologies are relevant as they offer safer, cost-effective, and highly engaging training solutions for sectors such as corporate training, healthcare education, and technical skills development, addressing the need for scalable and effective learning tools​ ​.

•  

The healthcare sector benefits significantly from immersive technologies in applications like surgical training, patient therapy, and medical imaging. This target market is relevant due to the increasing demand for innovative solutions that improve training efficiency, patient outcomes, and the overall quality of healthcare services​ ​.

•  

Enterprise and Remote Collaboration Enterprises use immersive technologies to enhance remote collaboration and productivity through tools like XR collaboration platforms. This market is relevant as businesses seek effective ways to maintain engagement and efficiency in remote and hybrid work models, making immersive workspaces a critical solution for modern workforce challenges​ ​.

nDisplay

nDisplay technology is used to drive large-scale, multi-display immersive environments.

It allows for seamless synchronization and rendering across multiple screens, making it ideal for complex installations and visualizations that require high levels of detail and interactivity.

We have a unique connection with nDisplay, having been early adopters of this technology.

Our involvement in a pioneering project, which demanded capabilities beyond the existing offerings, enabled us to influence the development and enhancement of nDisplay and Unreal Engine through our specific requirements.

Content creation tools

Our content creation tools include the Adobe suite, with Photoshop and Illustrator for graphic design, and After Effects and Premiere Pro for animation and video production, ensuring high-quality visual content.

For 3D authoring, we rely on Blender 3D and Cinema 4D. Blender provides comprehensive modelling and animation features, while Cinema 4D offers an intuitive interface and advanced animation tools.

Metashape is key for creating detailed 3D models from photographs, adding realism to our projects through photogrammetry.

TouchDesigner and Visual Studio are essential for building dynamic, interactive content and sophisticated applications, rounding out our robust toolkit for immersive experiences.

HANDHELD DEVICES

Physical Integration Technologies

We connect digital experiences with the physical world using motors, speakers, transducers, smoke, and haze machines.

These technologies enhance the realism and immersion of our projects, providing multi-sensory experiences that engage users on multiple levels.

04/06

TRADITIONAL DISPLAYS

Photogrammetry

Photogrammetry is the process of creating accurate 3D models from photographs by measuring and analyzing object positions within images. Our photogrammetry process uses Metashape, allowing us to capture real-world objects and environments with exceptional accuracy.

By leveraging Metashape, we convert high-resolution photos into precise 3D representations, essential for applications in architecture, engineering, virtual reality, and digital archiving. These detailed models add authenticity to our interactive content, making experiences more immersive.

Photogrammetry, powered by Metashape, is a crucial component of our toolkit, bringing real-world detail and precision to our digital creations and enhancing their realism and impact.

Project Process

Our creative process is rooted in an agile methodology, which allows us to be flexible and responsive to project needs.

Working within a small, multitalented in-house team, we emphasize rapid iteration and close collaboration. Here’s a breakdown of our approach:

05/01

Project Process

Agile Development

We make the creative possible by applying the “Recursive approach”, which means we work in cycles by breaking down challenges into smaller bite-size elements and then working through these in a structured way to deliver the best experience (which is where the name Recursive is born).

We limit our portfolio so that the team are involved in every project – they discuss each project, and often work together on them.

Agile helps us stay aligned with client needs and industry trends.

05/02

Project Process

Multitalented Team

Our team members are highly skilled across various disciplines, including VR/AR development, 3D modelling, animation, interactive design, and large-scale technically complex and diverse integration.

This versatility enables us to tackle complex projects efficiently and fosters a collaborative environment where innovative ideas thrive.

05/03

Project Process

Design Thinking

We incorporate design thinking into our process to ensure user-centric solutions.

This involves understanding the user’s needs, brainstorming creative solutions, prototyping, and testing.

By focusing on empathy and iterative refinement, we create immersive experiences that are both engaging and functional.

05/04

Project Process

Rapid Prototyping and Testing

We prioritise rapid prototyping to quickly bring concepts to life and test them in real-world scenarios.

This allows us to identify and address potential issues early, ensuring the final product is polished and effective. Continuous testing and feedback are integral to our development cycle.

05/05

Project Pocess

Integrated Technology Stack

Our projects leverage advanced tools such as Unreal Engine, nDisplay, 3D authoring software, and generative AI.

By integrating these technologies with physical elements like, large scale projection, winches, 3d Soundscapes, and atmospheric effects, we create comprehensive and immersive experiences that engage multiple senses.

05/05

Project Pocess

Client Collaboration

We work alongside you, keeping in very close communication throughout the project lifecycle providing regular updates, demonstrations, and feedback sessions to ensure that the final product outcome aligns with the vision and requirements, bringing the experience to life.

Conclusion

Our methodology blends agile development with design thinking principles, supported by a multitalented team and an integrated technology stack allowing us to provide a white glove experience for global projects large or small, our specialised long-serving team are genuinely passionate, taking personal pride and care to ensure the vision is successfully delivered, building a long-term relationship of trust and respect with the client and design team.
